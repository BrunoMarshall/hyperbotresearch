Discret

Invisible Utility for the Smart Home

Discret is designed to blend into everyday life. At first glance, it appears to be an ordinary table—but beneath the surface, it is a fully autonomous mobile robot.

Discret can move freely throughout the home, navigating to any area where it is needed. It integrates seamless communication between devices and is controlled through a dedicated app, enabling silent, unobtrusive assistance without disrupting daily life.

The goal is functionality without visibility: a robot that helps without being noticed.

Use Cases

Imagine a side table that moves to your bedside when you wake up, then relocates to your workspace when you start your day. A coffee table that positions itself perfectly for movie night, then retreats when you need floor space for exercise. Discret doesn't just respond to commands—it learns your routines and anticipates your needs.

In elderly care, Discret can serve as a mobile support surface, positioning itself near a person who needs stability. For smart homes, it acts as a mobile charging station or device hub. The applications are limited only by imagination—Discret is utility that adapts to life, rather than life adapting to furniture.
