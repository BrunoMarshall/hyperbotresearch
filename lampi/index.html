Lampi

Light, Exactly Where and When You Need It

Lampi is a mobile robotic lamp designed to deliver light dynamically. Instead of fixed lighting, Lampi moves to wherever illumination is required—automatically and on demand.

It integrates IoT connectivity and blockchain-based communication, enabling smart coordination with other devices and systems. Whether for efficiency, comfort, or automation, Lampi redefines lighting as a service rather than a static fixture.

Technical Specifications

  • Lighting: High-efficiency LED with variable color temperature
  • Brightness Range: 100-3000 lumens (adjustable)
  • Mobility: Omnidirectional autonomous navigation
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, LoRa, Blockchain protocols
  • Sensors: Ambient light detection, proximity sensors, environmental monitoring
  • Power: Rechargeable battery with automatic docking

The Future of Lighting

Lampi represents a fundamental shift in how we think about illumination. For over a century, lighting has been fixed—mounted to ceilings, attached to walls, stationary on desks. Lampi breaks free from this constraint. It doesn't just turn on and off; it understands context, anticipates needs, and positions itself with precision. Blockchain integration enables Lampi units to coordinate autonomously, creating dynamic lighting networks that respond to changing environments without centralized control. This is lighting that thinks, moves, and adapts—exactly as it should be.
